Receipts, not promises

Every sealed document ends with a court-ready audit.

The audit trail is the second-to-last page of every Sealed document. It is not a marketing claim — it is a printable, verifiable, cryptographically-anchored record of who signed what, when, and from where.

  • 01 Identity evidence Email verified, account-authenticated, IP and user-agent captured for every action.
  • 02 Cryptographic fingerprint SHA-256 hash of the file before and after signing. If a single byte changes, the seal breaks.
  • 03 eIDAS qualified timestamp Issued by a third-party trusted service provider — not by Sealed. Independently verifiable.
